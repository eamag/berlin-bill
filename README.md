# Berlin Bill

How Berlin Used Your 2025 Wage Tax.

[berlin-bill.eamag.me](https://berlin-bill.eamag.me)

## Overview

A visualization of Berlin's budget data, built to understand where tax money goes.

### Higher or Lower

Play Higher or Lower to guess which of two budget items has a higher or lower value.

## Development

- **Framework**: SvelteKit on Cloudflare Pages
- **Data Extraction**: GitIngest + Google AI Studio on Berlin's _Haushaltsdaten_
